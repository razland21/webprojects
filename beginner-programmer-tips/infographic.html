5

things beginner programmers need to know

according to Michelle Minkoff and Heather Billings

1 
Don't be afraid to ask for help.
2 
Don't try to generally learn the code, pick a project you like and try to emulate it.
3 
Don't be afraid of failure -- aim for it. You won't kill anyone.
4 
Have enough confidence to believe in yourself, but enough humility to know you don't know everything.
5 
Treat programming like a new beat. Know where to find the answers to the questions you know will arise.
